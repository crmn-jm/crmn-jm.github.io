About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher jointly affiliated with the BioSiP group at the University of Málaga and the SiPBA group at the University of Granada (Spain). My research lies at the intersection of neuroscience and machine learning, with a particular focus on neuroimaging and the development of AI-based methods for healthcare applications. I aim to design computational tools that can bridge the gap between research and real clinical practice..
